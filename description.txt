The official Cosmivengeon mod.

This mod adds the following:
1 Boss
3 NPCs
8 Weapons
2 Buffs/Debuffs
1 Accessory
1 New game mode (Desolation)
1 Piece of armour
1 Crafting material
6 Miscellaneous items

THIS IS THE ALPHA BUILD OF THE MOD.  ADDITIONAL THINGS ARE AS FOLLOWS:
- Being able to toggle Desolation Mode
- Two commands, "/setworldflag" and "/setmodflag"
